Mental side of a great recevier

Great eyes

This is an very important trait that you need to be a great recevier

The saying in football is that your "eyes" catch the ball

If you dont see the ball go into your hands then you will not be able to catch the ball to your full potential

Hand eye coordination drills are best to help and your attention to details

When doing ball drills or catching tennis balls, you want to keep your eyes attached to the ball all the way to the tuck

Footbal IQ

This trait can be natural but also can be developed

To help develop your IQ, you will need to watch a lot of film

You woud have to become a film junkie and love the process of it

Doing this will help you see things on the field before it actually happens and you will be able to react faster.

It is a process and not everyone likes to do this but we are here to talk about how to be a great wdie recevier, not a good one.

Being consistent with watching film helps you pick up certain details about your game that you may not have seen before and will aslo do the same for your opponent as well
