Great Hands

Having great hands is the most essential factor to being a recevier

Without these then it will be very difficult to play this position

There are various of drills that will help you devlop with having great hands

Drills

Catch tennis balls

You can throw the tennis ball of the wall to yourself

Have someone throw the tennis ball to you

We know cathing a tennis ball is not like a football and is easier to catch so be creative with it when cathing the ball.

The purpose of doing this drill is to concentrate on grabbing the ball and focusing on it.

Ball drills

Ball drills refers to catching a football while running or moving in different positions

This will help your ball skills a lot because you are getting used to catching and tracking the football

Having someone throw the ball to you is pereferd but if you can't find anyone then throwing to yourself is fine as well

This is also a good warm up before you start catching routes full speed

Juggs

This is a machine that shoots out footballs directly to you at any speed you adjust it to

It helps you create good habits with having strong hands while catching the ball and focusing on the nose of the football coming fast at you

Having this can help out a lot because it is not exaclty how a quaterback would throw to you but it is pretty close and will build your confidence catching the ball
